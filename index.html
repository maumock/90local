Why 90% Local?

Colorado’s marijuana taxes—$248M in 2023—promise school funding, but only 0.6% reaches education. Teachers strike while the state keeps 90% of sales tax. Flip it: 90% to counties for transparent, local control.

How It Works

AI-Powered Tracking

Use open data to show where every dollar goes—schools, health, or elsewhere.

Local Control

Counties manage 90% of taxes, ensuring funds reach classrooms, not state coffers.

Citizen Power

Transparent data empowers voters to hold politicians accountable.
